Why Choose Us

Why Choose NephroNexus

At NephroNexus, quality is never left to chance. From the water that enters our machines to the technology on which every session runs, each element of our process is chosen to give you cleaner, safer, and more consistent dialysis. Backed by German-engineered B Braun machines and an uncompromising standard of purification, we bring hospital-grade precision to Dalsinghsarai — so every patient receives care that is safe, reliable, and truly world-class.

Double Pass RO Water

Water is purified through a double-pass Reverse Osmosis system, removing impurities at every stage for the purest possible dialysis fluid.

Zero TDS Water

We maintain Zero TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) water quality, minimising risk and ensuring every session meets the highest safety standard.

German-Engineered B Braun Machines

Our advanced B Braun dialysis machines, made in Germany, deliver precise, hospital-grade performance and consistent, dependable treatment.

Wide Range of Dialyzers

We offer Low Flux, High Flux, and HDF dialyzers, allowing us to tailor every treatment precisely to each patient's clinical needs.

Our Services

Every Type of Dialysis.
Complete Kidney Care.

From routine haemodialysis to complex renal support, NephroNexus is equipped to handle the full spectrum of kidney care — all under one roof.

Haemodialysis (HD)

The most common form of dialysis — our HD sessions use modern high-flux machines to efficiently filter waste and excess fluid from the blood.

Peritoneal Dialysis (PD)

A gentler option that uses the peritoneum as a natural filter. Our team provides full training and support for patients managing PD at home.

Nocturnal Dialysis

Extended overnight haemodialysis sessions for gentler, more thorough toxin clearance — ideal for working patients who prefer treatment while they sleep.

SLEDD (Sustained Low-Efficiency Daily Diafiltration)

A hybrid therapy for critically ill and haemodynamically unstable patients, combining slower fluid/solute removal with daily sessions for gentler, sustained renal support.

Kidney Function Tests

Comprehensive lab investigations including serum creatinine, urea, eGFR, electrolytes, and urine analysis to monitor kidney health over time.

Renal Diet Counselling

Dietary guidance tailored to dialysis patients — managing potassium, phosphorus, protein, and fluid intake for the best possible outcomes.

Patient & Family Support

We support the whole family — education sessions, psychological well-being guidance, and a warm, welcoming environment for long-term dialysis patients.
